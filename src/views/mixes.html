DJ Mixes


In a previous life, I worked as a nightclub DJ in Santa Barbara, CA for about 7 years. These days, it's a hobby of mine. I like use a wide array of equipment from Technic MK3's to Pioneer CDJ's and other Dj Controllers all in conjunction with software such as Serato and sometimes Ableton Live. I started out spinning vinyl as a funky and disco House Dj in the early 2000's spinning at underground warehouse parties in the San Francisco Bay Area . Since then, I moved to mixing hip hop & R&B, top 40 , rock , mash-ups, trap, moombah , twerk , progressive, dub-step, trance , 80's & 90's jams, or any other tracks that get a dance floor to move. In any case, I see music and dance as a communal, fun way to get people together!
